\section *{Polynomial Multiplication Basics}
Consider, \\
$A(x) = 3x^2 + 2x + 5$ \\
$B(x) = 5x^2 + x + 2$ \\
\noindent
Here n = 3, A = (3, 2, 5) and B = (5, 1, 2). \\
n = largest degree of a polynomial + 1 \\ 
A = list of coefficents of polynomial A \\ 
B = list of coefficents of polynomial B \\
